Not enough satisfied yet in enjoying the moment as a wife, Ayu (Mawar De Jongh) suddenly becomes pregnant. Her personality changes completely. Usually, she has a passion for accompanying her husband Ditto (Adipati Dolken) everywhere, now she becomes lazy and worse: super sensitive. Ditto has to hold back and pretend not to be jealous of the attention Ayu gives to the fetus. The quarrels that often occur make Ayu and Ditto have to choose which one to keep; their marriage or the baby.
